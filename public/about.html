About Me

A Full Stack Developer is a versatile professional skilled in both front-end and back-end development, making them capable of handling the entire software development process. They work with front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create user interfaces, while managing back-end servers, databases, and APIs using languages such as Python, Java, or Node.js. Full Stack Developers play a crucial role in bridging the gap between user experience and technical functionality, ensuring seamless integration and performance of web or mobile applications. Their diverse expertise makes them highly sought after in the tech industry.
